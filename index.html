Welcome

Born and raised in Melbourne. I attended Penleigh Essendon Grammar School my entire schooling (k-12) and I am currently studying a Bachelor of Technology at RMIT. Besides a love of technology, I have a strong passion for cars and often cannot stop talking about them.

So where did it all begin?

My love of IT first began when I was only beginning to walk as I would use the Cassette player without any instruction. Ever since I have always been fascinated with the abilities that technology brings to our life’s. Learning to code during school was where my passion for IT really ignited where I was able to create a device capable of running an attendance log of students around the school. The system allowed me to explore my interest in cybersecurity and landed me a Mayoral Award in March 2019. Otherwise I have a broad range of IT skills that have been mostly self-taught with whatever I can get my hands on.

Where will it all end?

The dream job would eb a Security Software Engineer at Apple. Not only is this job at Apple but it is appealing to me because it involves the security aspect of IT which is such an important feature in today’s IT landscape. Futher a passion for cybersecurity will be met in this job role.
