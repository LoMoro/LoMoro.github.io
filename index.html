Current Project Idea

As security becomes a part of our everyday lives our keychain, virtual or physical continues to grow. As a person of tidiness and order I like to keep what I carry on me to a minimum to reduce clutter. However, somethings I just have to carry on me all the time such as keys for the home, office and car. Carrying so many keys can become a hassle and a solution to thing has to be found. Hence my idea to create one key that can give access to all of these things, reducing your keychain down to only a single key.

Arduino IDE

The software would run on Ardunio's IDE plateform. This is a widely supported platform that is even used in schools to teach kids to code.

End-To-End Encryption

In order to have a device that stores access to multiple things it must be secured using the latest encryption technology.

Easy To Setup

The aim of a single key system is to provide a more streamline experience in our day to day lives. You will notice a difference from the moment you turn it on.

Sharing A Key

Whether you need to share the key with family, friends or colleges. The system allows you to temporarily or perminantly give access to another user.
